The problem

Guiding students the old way is guesswork.

Spreadsheets, scattered browser tabs, and gut feel. It does not scale, and it leaves students with advice no one can stand behind.

01

Data lives everywhere

Tuition, deadlines, and admission criteria sit across hundreds of university sites. Keeping it current by hand is impossible.

02

No honest read on fit

Without real admission data, students apply where they have no chance, or miss a university that was a strong match.

03

Applications slip through

Essays, documents, and deadlines tracked in spreadsheets mean missed dates and students who quietly drop off.

04

Nothing to show for it

No pipeline, no outcomes, no proof of what worked. Every intake starts from zero.

Questions

Good to know.

Who is Ryogen built for?

Ryogen partners with schools, universities, and education organizations that help students apply to universities abroad. If you guide students through the study abroad journey, the workspace is built for you.

What does it cost our students?

Nothing. Ryogen is free for every student. That means more of the students you support can use it, without a cost barrier getting in the way.

Do we work inside Ryogen or our own tools?

Your team works inside the Ryogen workspace. You manage every student you guide in one dashboard, with their profiles, shortlists, documents, and deadlines all in one place, no spreadsheets on the side.

How is the university matching decided?

Every recommendation comes from SmartMatch, which ranks universities a student actually qualifies for with an honest fit score based on their profile. No inflated lists, no guesswork.

How do we get started?

Talk to our team and we will set up your workspace. Onboarding a cohort takes minutes, and there is no setup cost.
