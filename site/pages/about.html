About

Studying abroad, without the guesswork.

Ryogen is a free AI co-pilot that helps students find the universities they actually qualify for, and guides them through the whole application, at no cost.

Our mission

Make studying abroad a decision every student can make with confidence.

Studying abroad is one of the biggest decisions a student makes, and reliable, affordable guidance is almost impossible to find. Information is scattered, consultants are expensive and often conflicted, and no one tells a student their real chances. Ryogen replaces the consultant, the spreadsheet, and the guesswork with one honest co-pilot, so any student can make this decision well, regardless of where they start from.

What we believe

The principles behind Ryogen.

01

Honest, not optimistic

Fit scores reflect real admission data, not flattering guesses. We would rather tell you the truth than tell you what you want to hear.

02

Free, always, for students

Ryogen never charges the student. The people who need guidance most are the ones who can least afford it, so for them it stays free.

03

Global by default

Every part of Ryogen is built to add new destinations without being rebuilt. Wherever you want to study, the goal is to be there.

04

Self-serve first

You can go from your first search to a submitted application without ever speaking to a salesperson. The tools do the work.
